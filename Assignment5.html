Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Baahubali: The Beginning
    • Year Released: 2015
    • Main Actors: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah
    • Description: An epic action film that tells the story of two warring brothers in the kingdom of Mahishmati. The movie is known for its grand scale, visual effects, and compelling storyline.
  2. Geetha Govindam
    • Year Released: 2018
    • Main Actors: Vijay Deverakonda, Rashmika Mandanna
    • Description: A romantic comedy about an innocent young lecturer who falls in love with a woman who initially misunderstands and rejects him, leading to a series of humorous and heartwarming events.
  3. Pokiri
    • Year Released: 2006
    • Main Actors: Mahesh Babu, Ileana D'Cruz
    • Description: An action-packed story about an undercover cop infiltrating the underworld to bring down a mafia kingpin. The movie is renowned for its intense action sequences and gripping plot.
  4. iSmart Shankar
    • Year Released: 2019
    • Main Actors: Ram Pothineni, Nidhhi Agerwal, Nabha Natesh
    • Description: A science fiction action film about a street-smart hitman who has a change of heart and becomes a vigilante after a chip is implanted in his brain, allowing him to access someone else's memories.
  5. Sye
    • Year Released: 2004
    • Main Actors: Nithiin, Genelia D'Souza
    • Description: A sports drama centered around rugby, depicting the rivalry between two college gangs that unite to take on a common enemy in a high-stakes rugby match.
